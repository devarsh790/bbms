Blood Donation Process

A simple guide to your donation journey

The Donation Journey

1

Registration

Sign up and provide your basic information. Bring a valid ID and complete the registration form.

Duration: 5-10 minutes

2

Health Screening

A brief health questionnaire and mini-physical including blood pressure, temperature, and hemoglobin check.

Duration: 10-15 minutes

3

Donation

Relax while we collect approximately one pint of blood. You can read, listen to music, or chat with our staff.

Duration: 8-10 minutes

4

Refreshments

Enjoy snacks and drinks while resting for 10-15 minutes. This helps your body recover.

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Before Donation

  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a healthy meal
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid fatty foods
  • Bring valid ID
  • Wear comfortable clothing

During Donation

  • Relax and breathe normally
  • Tell staff if you feel unwell
  • Stay hydrated
  • Squeeze the stress ball
  • Enjoy the entertainment
  • Ask questions anytime

After Donation

  • Rest for 10-15 minutes
  • Drink extra fluids
  • Avoid heavy lifting (24h)
  • Keep bandage on (5h)
  • Eat iron-rich foods
  • Feel proud of yourself!
