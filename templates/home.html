Why Donate Blood?

Most people are able to give blood, but only 4% actually do. A single donation can save up to three lives.

Eligibility Requirements
  • Are fit and healthy
  • Weigh at least 50kg (110 lbs)
  • Are 17-66 years old
  • Have given blood in the last 2 years (if over 70)
